About Me

Hello there! (Insert Obi-Wan Kenobi gif or meme)

I’m Jordon. I’m a creative and an organizer who’s spent the last 10 years building a network of artists, learning about community, and holding various roles dedicated to challenging white-supremacy. Currently, I co-own a design studio called Blackbird Revolt. We center our design work on justice and liberation. More recently, I’ve taken an interest in coding. The fist time I looked through any code was during the prework for coding bootcamp. It was a fascinating and thrilling peak into the world of a full-stack developer. I knew it would be challenging, but I didn’t realize how engaging all the content would be. Currently, our bootcamp is exploring Javascript. This makes HTML and CSS look like a walk in the park. I am looking forward to learning more and seeing how I can contribute to the world as a developer.
