Blackbird Revolt

Promoting social change through conscious creativity.

The Story

In 2017, I Co-founded Blackbird Revolt. They acts as a platform for conscious creatives looking to transform communities. They find ways to support the causes they care about, while providing opportunities for others to do the same. Through design, and other forms of art, Blackbird Revolt aims to inspire people to engage in activism and movement work.

The Work

Art and design have always been essential throughout their lives and to the movements that have helped shape them. Blackbird Revolt believes that their work can never be separate from community. They are dedicated to centering community and a collective vision for the future.
