Introversion

Introversion is an app that helps introverts navigate awkward social environments. This app will generate jokes and news that will allow the user to engage in dreadful small talk.


Introversion was initially developed as a class project for coding boot camp. The founding developers are Esmond Kim, Dillon Vora, Jess Parnow, Spencer Johnson, and myself. We intend to continue developing this app to support introverts in social settings. We intend to add additional features that would make the app more useful, engaging, and easy to use.
